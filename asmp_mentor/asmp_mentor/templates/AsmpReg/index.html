ABOUT
The Placement Mentoring Program aims at providing mentorship in various core and non-core fields such as Finance, Analytics, Consultancy, IT/Software etc. for the final year students in order to aid them in their placement process. The program accomplishes this through the contributions of the recently graduated batches who have gone through the same placement process and are, therefore, well versed with the challenges one might face during the process. The program serves as a medium to connect the mentor-mentee pair where the latter finds a helping hand to discuss the strategies to be employed while seeking placement in a particular field. PMP initiates the first interaction between the mentor and mentees which they can carry forward at their own pace and as per requirements, with intermediate follow-ups to overcome any hurdles that may appear.

FAQ

Why should you take the guidance of a mentor under PMP?

Your mentor has gone through the same placement procedure that you are going to go through. In these critical times, even a little help goes a long way. Having a mentor helps to streamline all the various thoughts you have and clears your doubts. Everything considered the guidance of a mentor is an essential thing that can help you crack placements.

What is the process after registration for a mentee?

After the registration is completed, a mentor will be allocated to you based on your preferences and SOPs. You have to initiate the conversation with your mentor, and then you can mutually decide on the way ahead. We will have a few follow-ups to assist you through any difficulties.

What will be the use of my personal details?

Details such as name, roll number, and contact details will remain confidential till the allocation procedure. Only the allocated mentor will know your contact details.

What is different in PMP from last time?

  • Mentees will have an option to fill in their preferences for the mentor (based on the work profile).
  • The allocation will also allow cross-department mentor-mentee pairing

How was the experience of last year's mentees from this program?

Based on the feedback received, the mentees were highly benefited from the program last year. The guidance they got from their mentors helped them throughout the placement process. They got to learn from their mentor’s experience, and all the valuable tips and tricks helped them crack placements and the overall process.

INSTRUCTIONS
  • After opening the site, click on the register button.
  • After clicking on it, fill in your personal information and your statement of purpose for applying in a particular field, and then click on next. If you are applying for mentors from different fields, make sure to show your interest in all those fields in the SoP.
  • After clicking on next, you will be directed to a window containing mentors’ profiles and their fields. You are allowed to select five mentors overall in preferential order (you can choose mentors in any area).
  • It is advised to choose a mentor from your parent department so as to avail the maximum benefits of the program. Also note that chances of being allotted a mentor from the same department are higher
  • The allocation of mentors is based on first-come, first-serve in the first round, and based on SOPs in the second round. After the threshold amount of mentees chooses a specific mentor, their card will no longer be displayed on the registration portal.
